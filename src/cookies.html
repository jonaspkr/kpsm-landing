Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and can provide information to the site owners. We also use localStorage, which functions similarly but stores data directly in your browser.

How we use cookies

This website uses minimal cookies and browser storage. We categorise them as follows:

Name Category Purpose Duration Provider
cookie_consent Essential Stores your cookie consent preference so we don't show the banner again Persistent (localStorage) KPSM (first-party)
Google Fonts CSS Essential Loads the Montserrat typeface used on this site. Google may log IP addresses per their privacy policy. Session Google

We do not currently use analytics, marketing, or advertising cookies. If we add analytics in the future, they will only load after you explicitly consent.

How to control cookies in your browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close the browser

Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.

Changes to this policy

We will update this policy if we add new cookies or change how we use existing ones. The "last updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.
